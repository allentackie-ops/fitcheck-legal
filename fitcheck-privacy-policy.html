Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Effective immediately

00 Overview

FitCheck ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered personal styling and social fashion app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use FitCheck.

By using FitCheck, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to being transparent about how we handle your data.

We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your wardrobe and style data is used solely to provide and improve your personal styling experience.

01 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — Name, email address, username, date of birth, and profile photo when you create an account
  • Wardrobe data — Photos and metadata of clothing items you scan into your wardrobe
  • Content you create — Posts, stories, comments, voice notes, and messages you share on FitCheck
  • Style preferences — Occasions, style tags, body type, and gender identity you provide for personalised recommendations
  • Usage data — How you interact with the app, including features used, posts viewed, and time spent
  • Device information — Device type, operating system, push notification token, and app version
  • Location data — Approximate location when you use location-based outfit recommendations (only when permitted)
  • Contacts — Phone numbers from your contacts to help you find friends on FitCheck (only when you grant permission; we do not store your contact list)

02 How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate personalised AI outfit recommendations from your wardrobe
  • Display your profile, posts, and stories to other users according to your privacy settings
  • Send you push notifications for messages, follows, reactions, and other social activity
  • Process your Pro subscription through Apple's In-App Purchase system
  • Improve our AI models and app features (using anonymised, aggregated data only)
  • Enforce our Community Guidelines and respond to reports of abuse
  • Send transactional emails about your account (subscription confirmations, security notices)

03 AI & Wardrobe Data

FitCheck uses Claude AI (by Anthropic) to analyse clothing photos and generate outfit recommendations. When you scan a clothing item:

  • The image is sent to Anthropic's API for clothing identification
  • Anthropic processes the image in accordance with their privacy policy
  • We store the resulting clothing metadata (name, type, colour, style) in your wardrobe
  • Your wardrobe data is private by default — only you can see it unless you choose to share posts
We do not use your wardrobe photos to train AI models. Your clothing images are processed for identification and then stored securely for your personal use only.

04 Sharing Your Information

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Other FitCheck users — Content you post publicly (posts, stories, profile) is visible to other users according to your privacy settings
  • Service providers — We use Supabase (database), Anthropic (AI), RevenueCat (subscriptions), Resend (email), and Expo (app infrastructure). These providers process data only as necessary to deliver our service
  • Legal requirements — We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users
  • Business transfers — If FitCheck is acquired or merges with another company, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties for marketing purposes.

05 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS
  • Database access is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) — users can only access their own data
  • Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth with secure token management
  • Payment processing uses Apple's native In-App Purchase system — we never handle your payment card details

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

06 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information via your profile settings
  • Delete your account and all associated data (Settings → Delete Account)
  • Export your wardrobe data by contacting us
  • Withdraw consent for optional features such as location access and push notifications at any time via your device settings
  • Disable your account temporarily without deleting your data (Settings → Disable Account)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@fitcheck.social

07 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Your profile, posts, wardrobe, and messages are permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Some data may be retained longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g. fraud prevention records)
  • Anonymised, aggregated analytics data may be retained indefinitely

08 Children's Privacy

FitCheck is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@fitcheck.social and we will delete that information.

Users aged 13–17 should review this policy with a parent or guardian.

09 Cookies & Tracking

FitCheck is a mobile application and does not use browser cookies. We use the following for app functionality:

  • Local storage — To keep you logged in and cache your preferences on your device
  • Analytics — Anonymised, aggregated usage data to understand how features are used and improve the app

We do not use third-party advertising trackers or cross-app tracking.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via in-app notification or email at least 7 days before changes take effect. Continued use of FitCheck after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

The date at the top of this page indicates when this policy was last updated.
